Bubbling Mode

Tile

  • ↑, ↓, ←, → to move
  • Ctrl Z to undo
  • Ctrl Y or Ctrl Shift Z to redo
  • Enter to record a change to the board
  • Delete or Backspace to delete a tile

Board

  • Ctrl Z to undo
  • Ctrl Y or Ctrl Shift Z to redo
  • Shift Enter to spawn a new tile
