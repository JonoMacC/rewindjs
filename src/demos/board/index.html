Demo 3

Board

The board is rewindable. Tiles can be added, removed, and rearranged. Each change can be undone or redone.

Tile

  • ↑, ↓, ←, → to move
  • a-z, 0-9 to change label
  • Ctrl Z to undo
  • Ctrl Y or Ctrl Shift Z to redo
  • Delete or Backspace to delete a tile

Board

  • Ctrl Z to undo
  • Ctrl Y or Ctrl Shift Z to redo
  • Shift Enter to insert a new tile
